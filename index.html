Welcome To

Delpha Education



Delpha Education is an educational platform to assist students in
their academics and ace their examinations through the power of
knowledge and curiosity. Our collection of high-quality resources
including notes, practice questions, as well as videos aid students in
being able to maximize and reach their true potential.






MISSION


To inspire and empower students around the world through the
power of curiosity.



VISION


To provide quality education and impact
millions of learners globally.

































Learning Notes



Learn and revise different concepts from all topics and units
through our specially designed learning notes, which are divided
per class and written by top students.












Practice Problems



We provide practice materials (quizzes, revision questions,
tips and tricks) in our notes as well as in Google Forms for you
to be able to test what you have learned!












Welcome to the Team



Interested in becoming a part of our team?

  • Join as a contributor, where you will create quality subject-specific
    notes as well as practice problems.


  • Join as a manager, where you will become a part of our leadership
    team and be in charge of the four different aspects of the organization:
    Content, Growth & Development, Tech & Maintenance, and Community Support.
